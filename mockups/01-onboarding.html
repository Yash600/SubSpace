Design Decisions
⚡
3 Steps Maximum
Research shows drop-off spikes after 3 onboarding screens. We stay within that limit.
🎯
Role-Based Personalisation
Step 1 answer changes what the user sees in Steps 2 & 3. Buyers see savings. Sellers see earnings.
🔁
One-Time Only
Stored in user preferences. Never interrupts returning users. Skip always available.
