5 Pillars Summary
PILLAR 01
UX
7Ps — Product & Process
No first-run onboarding, checkout date bug, broken gift card pages, contradictory vendor states, Money Saved buried 3 clicks deep.
PILLAR 02
Features / Services
SWOT — Weaknesses
Only 8 trackable subscriptions. Negotiate API marketed but absent. Bill Payments empty in primary nav.
PILLAR 03
GTM & ICPs
7Ps — People & Promotion
3 user types, 0 role-based onboarding. Privacy policy copied from Amazon's template — DPDP 2023 risk.
PILLAR 04
Competitor Analysis
Porter's Five Forces
Real competitor is WhatsApp+UPI (free, trusted, zero friction). No trust layer to beat informal sharing.
PILLAR 05
Potential Collaborations
SWOT — Opportunities
Refer & Earn exists but no campus program, no corporate benefits integration, no UPI refund partnership.
